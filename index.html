Tour Guide Autonomous Agent

Project Description

What we're doing!

Campus tours are a very important part of the admission process at Connecticut College, playing an important role in attracting applicants and decreasing the schoolâ€™s acceptance rate. In order to modernize this ancient tradition, we plan to develop an autonomous agent called CoCoGo that will be able to lead parts of campus tours. The agent will engage with the visitors on campus, give historical and current information about buildings and spaces on campus, and make sure to have an personalized speed based on the crowd so it can lead the visitors accordingly given their speed. The agent will be equipped with autonomous movement, consisting of a body made of light metal and placed on wheels. The idea is to either build the body of the robot from scratch or purchase some sort of automatized wagon that we can build upon. While the agent moves towards the destination, it will use sensors to avoid both human and non-human obstacles, and a camera to turn around and identify humans on the tour. We will experiment with using Raspberry Pi as the controller. Along with this, besides the speech component to the robot we want to add features so that it has personality and can interact with visitors through verbal communication and gestures, which will enhance the experience of the people on the tour.
